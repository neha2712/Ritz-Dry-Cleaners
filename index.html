About

More than just dry cleaning

At Ritz Dry Cleaners, each garment is individually evaluated to determine the safest and most effective method of cleaning. For many high-end and delicate items, dry cleaning is the best option to clean and restore them. For optimal results, each piece is uniquely treated to remove a wide variety of stains, which include grease, lipstick, ink, blood, food, coffee, wine and even toothpaste!

Wedding Gown

We have perfected the art and science of wedding dress cleaning. We carefully inspect each dress and make sure that we identify all areas that need cleaning or repair. We restore and even hand wash, if necessary, each wedding gown and make sure that all stains are removed thoroughly.

Corporate

When it comes to commercial dry cleaning services, Ritz provides effective solutions to meet the needs of businesses and corporations. We work with wide a variety of businesses, including hotel chains, restaurants, retail stores and professional offices. We pride ourselves in delivering superior quality and reliable service in helping to meet the needs of your business on a daily basis.

Alteration

Our skilled alteration team provides full capabilities for garment alterations and garment repair services. Our services include replacing lost buttons or snaps, repairing zippers, reattaching linings, repairing loose hems, mending split seams, and more.

Bedding & Table Linen

Whether cleaning your down-filled comforter or professionally laundering and pressing your table linens for holiday dinners we have the on-site facilities to clean the majority of your household items.
